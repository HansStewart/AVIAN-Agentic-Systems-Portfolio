Overview

This site was built as a professional demonstration environment for three AI automations designed for AVIAN's industrial sales and marketing motion. It presents the systems as an integrated operating layer rather than a collection of disconnected workflows.

The site is designed for executive review, technical evaluation, and recruiting review. It gives a recruiter, CEO, or internal team a clear way to understand what was built, how each automation works, what each system produces, and how the systems perform together inside a unified command center experience.

What the site includes

Three automation sections with architecture explanations and output previews

Embedded Loom walkthroughs for each automation

A live-style dashboard showing account scoring, content quality, and re-engagement prioritisation

A results section showing measurable operational output

A premium visual presentation aligned to AVIAN branding and positioning

Automations featured

Account Intelligence Engine

A lead intelligence system that ingests target accounts, enriches them with live research, scores them against AVIAN's ideal customer profile, and generates outreach-ready messaging for high-fit opportunities.

Content Generation Pipeline

A governed content system that accepts a structured brief, extracts live brand voice context, generates channel-specific content assets, grades them across four quality dimensions, and routes approved assets into a content library.

Re-Engagement System

A behavioural monitoring system that tracks dormant prospects, prioritises contacts based on inactivity and original fit, and generates contextual follow-up messaging so high-value opportunities do not go cold unnoticed.
